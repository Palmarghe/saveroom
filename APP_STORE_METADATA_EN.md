## Description

SaveRoom is an independent premium guide and save planning app for users who want to manage their career saves more systematically.

The app organizes team selection, squad planning, transfer priorities, tactical issues, scouting filters, finance management, academy development, matchday decisions, and crisis recovery into clear decision departments.

Key features:

- Generate save ideas with Save Wizard
- Explore 20 decision departments
- 100+ coherent guide entries
- Track season goals in Plans
- Save favorite guides
- Choose from 10 premium themes
- Production-quality Turkish and English language support
- Independent guide structure without official game data

SaveRoom is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Football Manager, Sports Interactive, or SEGA. It does not use official logos, official databases, or scraped game data.
