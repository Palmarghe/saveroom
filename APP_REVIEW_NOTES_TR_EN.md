## English

SaveRoom is an independent manager guide and save planning app.

Important notes:

- It is not affiliated with Football Manager, Sports Interactive, or SEGA.
- It does not use official logos or official databases.
- In the v5.5 package, the Pro/paywall layer is demo architecture; no real payment is processed.
- User-entered profile, plan, and favorite data are stored locally on the device.
- The app does not use ads, tracking, or third-party analytics.

Testing:

- The Home screen appears after launch.
- The Save Wizard tab generates a career plan.
- The Discover screen includes 20 decision departments and 100+ guides.
- The Pro tab displays demo Pro status.
- The Profile screen can create a local demo profile.
